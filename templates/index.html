How to access the Database


Register with Us

Register a user with our application at access our reporting platoform to help your organization gain access to data on prosecutorial races across the United States.

Get Approval

Once registered our the Prosecutor Database team will vet your application and grant your organizations users access to the database reports and query builder. Please note that approval can take up to 48 hours.

Start Querying

Once you have been approved you can access ready made reports on our user database, as well as a report builder to help your organization identify which candidates, elections, and district show promise for change.
